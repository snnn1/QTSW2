## Root Cause Hypotheses

1. Multi-day carryover is occupying fresh 2026-05-12 stream journals with prior-day slot instances. This explains missed `YM1`, `ES2`, and `NG2` fresh trades.
2. Fresh 2026-05-12 terminal intent verification does not commit or terminalize the matching stream journal. This explains 9 fresh streams remaining `RANGE_LOCKED` and the summary count of 12 incomplete streams.
3. Ownership snapshot emission or summary aggregation is reading stale carryover snapshots after completed reentry exits. This explains stale `MES`/`MNG`/`MYM` active rows and inflated `ownership_journal_open_qty_at_shutdown`.
4. Playback evidence mixes event-clock timestamps and wall-clock timestamps. This makes slot-time reasoning fragile unless every audit table separates event time from observed/log time.
